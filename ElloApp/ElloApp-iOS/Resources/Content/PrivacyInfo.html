Account deletion information

We understand that you're looking to delete your Ello Messenger account, and we're here to guide you through the necessary steps. Before proceeding, please ensure that you have withdrawn your leftover balance, canceled any active subscriptions, transferred ownership or deleted paid channels that you created, as these actions may affect your account closure process. Additionally, it's important to note that any products or services purchased within the app will be lost when you delete your account. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Withdraw Your Leftover Balance:

    • Navigate to your Ello Pay.
    • Locate the option to withdraw My balance or My earnings.
    • Follow the on-screen instructions to initiate the withdrawal process.
    • Make sure your balance is fully withdrawn before proceeding to the next step.

  2. Cancel Your Active Subscriptions:

    • Look for the "Subscriptions" section.
    • Identify and cancel any active subscriptions associated with your account.
    • Follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.
    • Ensure all subscriptions are successfully cancelled.

  3. Cancel Subscriptions Made with Apple or Google:

    • If you have subscribed to Ello Messenger through Apple (iOS) or Google (Android), please note that these subscriptions are managed directly through your respective app stores.
    • To cancel an iOS subscription:
    • On your iOS device, go to Settings.
    • Scroll down and tap "iTunes & App Store."
    • Tap your Apple ID at the top of the screen.
    • Tap "View Apple ID" and sign in if prompted.
    • Tap "Subscriptions."
    • Find the Ello Messenger subscription and cancel it.
    • To cancel an Android subscription:
    • Open the Google Play Store.
    • Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the upper-left corner.
    • Tap "Subscriptions."
    • Find the Ello Messenger subscription and cancel it.
    • Once canceled, your subscription through Apple or Google will no longer renew.

  4. Transfer Ownership or Delete Paid Channels:

    • If you've created paid channels (subscription or Course), consider transferring ownership to another/admin user or deleting them.
    • To transfer ownership:
    • Go to the channel's settings.
    • Find the option to transfer ownership.
    • Select the new owner and confirm the transfer.
    • To delete a channel:
    • Navigate to the channel's settings.
    • Locate the option to delete the channel.
    • Follow the steps to permanently remove it.

After you have completed these steps, your account should be free of any active subscriptions, paid channels, and leftover balance. Once everything is in order, you can proceed with the account deletion process.

Please be aware that when you delete your Ello Messenger account, any products or services purchased within the app will be lost, and you won't be able to recover them. Make sure to consider this before proceeding with the deletion.

It's essential to take these steps carefully and thoughtfully to ensure a smooth transition. If you encounter any issues or have further questions, don't hesitate to contact our customer support for assistance.

We hope this guide helps you delete your Ello Messenger account smoothly. If you ever decide to return, you're always welcome back.
